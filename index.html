Bio

Hi. I'm Mus! This is me up here. I moved to England from Turkey in 2011. I worked at a few places before going to Manchester Metropolitan University.

After completing my degree, I started working at Heywood Pension Technologies. Here I spent 3.5 years working on various projects as a Java develop.

After developing grave interest in the role, I took the raised opputunity to become a Scrum Master at Heywood. I have been working as a Scrum Master since then. It has been a journey with many turns, ups and downs as the company transformed from a pension admin provider to the giant pension and finance technology provider it is today.

It has grown from 100 employees and a handfull of development teams to nearly 300 employees and over 12 development teams who are working on various products and technologies.

In the recent months, I have been working with a team that was developing a new product using front-end technologies more predominantly than ever.

This exposure to these new technologies such as HTML, CSS, JavaScript and React has sparked back a light in me.

This was the developer enthusiast in me, calling me to tell me that this is what I should be doing.

I immedietly enrolled to an online course to teach myself these languages to compliment my creativity and problem solving skills.
